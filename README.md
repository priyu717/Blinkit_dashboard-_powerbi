# 🛒 Blinkit Sales Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

##  Project Overview

This project presents an interactive sales analysis dashboard for Blinkit (India’s last-minute grocery delivery app) using Power BI.
The dashboard provides insights into sales performance, customer behavior, and outlet-level trends to support data-driven decision-making.

---

##  Objectives

* Analyze overall sales performance of Blinkit
* Identify top-performing product categories and outlet types
* Understand customer preferences (fat content, item types)
* Compare sales across different outlet locations (Tier 1, Tier 2, Tier 3)
* Track trends over time for business growth insights

---

##  Tools & Technologies Used

* Power BI (Data Visualization & Dashboarding)
* Excel / CSV Dataset (Data Source)
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions for calculations)

---

## Dashboard Features

###  Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* **Total Sales**: Overall revenue generated
* **Average Sales**: Average revenue per transaction
* **Total Items**: Total number of items sold
* **Average Rating**: Customer satisfaction score

---

###  Visual Analysis

####  Outlet Analysis

* Sales distribution by **Outlet Size** (Small, Medium, High)
* Performance comparison across **Outlet Locations (Tier 1, 2, 3)**
* Outlet Type performance (Grocery Store, Supermarket Types)

---

####  Product Analysis

* Sales by **Item Type** (Fruits, Snacks, Dairy, etc.)
* Comparison of **Low Fat vs Regular products**
* Category-wise contribution to total sales

---

#### 📈 Trend Analysis

* Sales trend based on **Outlet Establishment Year**
* Growth pattern and peak performance periods

---

#### 📊 Tabular Insights

* Detailed breakdown of:

  * Total Sales
  * Total Items
  * Average Sales
  * Average Rating
    by outlet type

##  Key Insights

* Tier 3 locations generate the highest sales compared to Tier 1 and Tier 2
* Medium-sized outlets contribute the most to overall sales
* Regular fat products dominate sales compared to low-fat items
* Top-performing categories include Fruits, Snacks, and Household items
* Sales show fluctuations based on outlet establishment trends
